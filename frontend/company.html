About Us

Who Are We?

ANCRZ is a newly established transport company located at Valero St. Poblacion, San Rafael, Bulacan. Our primary focus is on domestic transport services, specifically door-to-door delivery and warehouse-to-warehouse transfer. With our fleet of eight (8) 6-wheeler closed vans, which is continuously expanding, we are able to meet the transportation needs of our clients effectively. Additionally, we have established a network of fifty (50) units from our suppliers, including a range of vehicles from AUVs to 10-wheeler wing vans, ensuring that we can handle various types of cargo and provide flexible solutions.

ANCRZ is led by a team of dedicated professionals who ensure the smooth functioning of our operations. Joemarie Dalipe serves as our General Manager, overseeing the overall management of the company. Ann Cruz-Dalipe takes charge of finance and human resources as the Finance & Human Resource Manager, ensuring that we maintain a stable and productive work environment. Remedios Joson manages our day-to-day operations as the Operations Manager, ensuring efficiency and timely execution of tasks. Lastly, John Mark Benito, as the Business Development Manager, focuses on expanding our clientele and fostering valuable partnerships.
